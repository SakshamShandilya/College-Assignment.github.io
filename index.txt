# about

About me

Saksham is a creative person who knows how to maximize efficiency by organizing things efficiently. Both professionally and personally, he is adept at handling exceptional situations.

He has a 'can-do attitude', a strong sense of responsibility, a love of challenging projects as well as an innovative outlook. Saksham loves to break boundaries and always brings an innovative perspective to the table.

He is a kind of early bird who develops in the morning and loves mentoring in the evening. He has an entrepreneurial mindset and not only works on his startup but also ideates others. Even though he is just a programmer on an average day, he knows his skills can positively impact lives. He is also an entrepreneur by nature as he loves working on converting ideas and thoughts into visual pieces. 

Saksham is skilled in Data Structures and problem-solving, and his career goal is to solve a real-world problem with his problem-solving skills.

As he expands his network and adds meaningful value to his career, he is open to mentors, guides, and potential co-founders. 
He is also looking forward to collaborating on projects and learning new skills together. He takes pride in being a team player.
He is also open to tips on interview preparation, building a personal brand, launching a start-up, and he needs productivity tips.

He is always open to new opportunities and would love to have a chat over coffee.

My work includes  building your website so that it is functional and user-friendly but at the same time attractive. Moreover, I add personal touch to your product and make sure that is eye-catching and easy to use. My aim is to bring across your message and identity in the most creative way. I created web design for many famous brand companies.
